README file for weather data for 
Pepke et al. (2022) Causes and consequences of variation in early-life telomere length in a bird metapopulation, Ecology and Evolution.

Below is a short explanation of variables in each coloumn. For details see the Methods section in the main text.
NA indicates missing data.

date : date of weather measurement
year :	year of weather measurement
month :	month of weather measurement
day : day of weather measurement
yearday : numbered day of the year of weather measurement
temperature : daily mean temperature in K 
precipitation :	total daily amount of precipitation in mm
atm_pressure : mean daily atmospheric pressure in hPa
NAO : daily North Atlantic Oscillation (NAO) index
